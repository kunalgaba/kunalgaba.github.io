Frequently Asked Questions

I have changed the security on my property - do I need to let you know?

If you are altering the security on your property you should inform us as it may change the conditions of your home insurance. This may affect your premium.

I'm about to install a new kitchen and renovate my bathroom - should I notify Allianz?

Before commencing renovations it is important that you notify us as it may affect your coverage and the terms and conditions of your home insurance policy. It is important that you increase your sum insured to reflect any extensions or renovations to make sure you are adequately covered in the event of a home insurance claim.
